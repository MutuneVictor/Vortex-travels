Details

Maasai Mara is one of the most famous national reserves in Kenya, located in the southwestern part of the country. It is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including lions, cheetahs, elephants, and giraffes. The annual wildebeest migration is also a popular attraction, where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles move between Tanzania and Kenya in search of fresh grazing land.

Activities

  • Game drives to see the Big Five
  • Hot air balloon rides over the savannah
  • Cultural visits to Maasai villages
  • Nature walks and birdwatching

Accommodation

Ngao Adventures offers a range of accommodation options in Maasai Mara, from luxury tented camps to budget-friendly lodges. All of our accommodations are located within the reserve and offer stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
