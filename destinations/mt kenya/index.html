Mount Kenya

Details


Mount Kenya is the second-highest mountain in Africa, and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. It is located in central Kenya, about 150km northeast of Nairobi. Mount Kenya is a popular destination for hiking and climbing enthusiasts, and there are several routes to the summit.


Difficulty Level

The hike up Mount Kenya is considered to be challenging and requires a good level of fitness. It is recommended that hikers be in good health and have some hiking experience before attempting to climb the mountain.


What to Bring

  • Hiking boots
  • Warm clothing
  • Rain gear
  • Sunscreen
  • Water bottle
  • Snacks
